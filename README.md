# cfb26-dc
Defensive Scheme Builder for CFB 26
If you commonly place other users in dynasty & you know their offensive tendenacies...this is likely for you.
1. On the main screen, input your scouting report of the given player.
2. Hit the "Build game plan" button
3. You will be brought to the DC page
4. You can use this during a game or use it to prepare
5. Select the personnel & formation for a given offensive play call
6. You will receieve multiple defensive options based on your selections and the formation/personnel
7. Select the formation and you will see coverage selections
8. Once you have selected the formation you will also see adjustments, coaching adjustments and a play call sheet
9. Enjoy!
